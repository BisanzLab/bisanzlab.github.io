For over 20 years, the Highly Cited Researchers program has used citation metrics and other indicators to identify influential researchers across the world based on their last 11 years of publishing. This year Jordan makes his first appearance on the list which also includes frequent collaborator Andrew Patterson and fellow One Health Microbiome Center member Francisco Dini-Andreote. Read more and see the list [here](https://clarivate.com/academia-government/blog/highly-cited-researchers-celebrating-research-excellence-in-2025/).
